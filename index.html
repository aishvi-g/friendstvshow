An American Sitcom about lives of 6 friends across 10 seasons with highest popularity ever! Following the lives of these six reckless adults living in Manhattan, as they indulge in adventures which make their lives both troublesome and happening.

Characters

Chandler Bing

Chandler Bing, the King of Sarcasm is one of the most relatable character of the show. He is a guy who does "something wiht numbers" for living,sometimes referered as "Chanandler Bong" , uses humour as a defence mechanism and doesn't have a great dating experience ends up having the best love life we all dream of!!!! But with Who? Well, thats for you to find out! Played by Matthew Perry, this character is certainly favourite of many fans for his puns and punches.

Ross Geller

Ross Geller, a paleontologist obsessed with dinosaurs. His hobby? "Marriage". Yes! he is mariied thrice in the show. He is the apple of the eye of his parents and elder brother of Monica Geller and bestfriends with Chandler Bing since college. A true lover who has been in love with a friend since 9 years. Watch out to see what happens to him and his crush. This character played by David Schwimmer is certainly a witty, funny and one of its kind character.

Monica Geller

You think you got OCD? No my friend, not more than Monica. A chef by profession but an over-organized person by nature. She is the little sister of Ross who wasn't so little till her high school but now after losing a huge number of pounds has become a very fine lady. Marriange, kids is a yes for her but not for the guys she dates until she lands with a really awesome one. The funny story is how? Watch this role played by Courtney Cox and I can bet you will fall in love with her.

Rachel Green

Dad's spoilt little girl? Yes thats how the character of Rachel Green starts but she is a characters who grows most of all. The fashionsta of the show , literally as she works some of the most famous fashion brands of all times. Best friends with Monica since high school she is becomes the last addition to the group. Her mean girl attitude as well over caring and ready to cry instnct spice up the show. Known for her charms this role is played by Jennifer Aniston .

Joey Tribbiani

"How you doin'?" is all it takes for Joey to sweep a girl off her feet. An italian origin extremely good looking guy who doesn't commit to anyone , has passion for acting lands up in Manhattan to try his luck in acting indutry and becomes the roomate of Chandler. What he respects most is his friendship and his food as "Joey doesn't share food". This character played by Matt Le Blanc may not be the smartest but one of the most humourous character of the show.

Phoebe Buffay

A blond masseues who loves playing guitar , she has her way of own to live. Coming from a poor background where her mother killed herself and her twin sister "Ursula" is one weird creature , Phoebe has a charm of her own. She is an ex-roomate of Monica with an overly positve attitude. She is friend who can scold you,teach you french if you may or even beat you. Standing strong by her morals this role played by Lisa Kudrow is very charismatic.
